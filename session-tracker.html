Fingers — Ramp sets

Find your working weight.

Two hands on a 20mm edge, feet on the floor. Hang for seven seconds, rest about a minute between hangs. Gradually increase the load each hang.

Once it feels heavy but you're still a few seconds from failure — that's your working weight. Log each hang as you go.

Bouldering

Now you can enjoy it.

The strength work is done. Keep it controlled rather than all-out — it'll reduce injury risk and help you move better. Forget the grades.

The stimulus matters, not your ego, or trying to impress that cute person at the wall who almost certainly isn't paying attention anyway.

Pull-ups

3 sets of 6. Leave some in the tank.

Only if you've still got something left — don't force it. Stop with around three reps still in the tank. Add weight if bodyweight feels easy.

Two minutes rest between sets. Timer starts when you tick each set off.
